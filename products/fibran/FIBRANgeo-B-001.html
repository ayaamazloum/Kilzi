FIBRANgeo Β-001

FIBRANgeo B-001 is a semi-rigid stone wool insulation board made by spinning molten mineral rock into fibers, using environmentally friendly electric furnaces. It meets European Standard EN 13162 for mineral wool insulation and offers outstanding thermal, acoustic, and fire-resistant performance. Treated with resins and hydrophobic compounds, it is water-repellent, non-hygroscopic, and chemically inert.

Key Benefits

  • Excellent thermal and sound insulation
  • Non-combustible with high fire resistance
  • Water-repellent and breathable (µ=1)
  • Lightweight, easy to handle and install
  • Chemically neutral, resistant to vibrations, pests, and microorganisms
  • Environmentally friendly and recyclable

Technical Specifications

Available in various thicknesses (20–160 mm) with standard board dimensions of 600 x 1200 mm. Packaged in PE film and palletized.

Applications

Used for insulation in vertical and horizontal building elements such as:

  • Cavity walls (masonry, drywalls, ventilated facades)
  • Roofs, ceilings, attics, and floors
  • Construction of insulating panels and fire-rated doors

Other dimensions can be provided upon request.
