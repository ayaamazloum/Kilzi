FIBRANgeo R-001-KO

FIBRANgeo R-001-KO is a stone wool wired mat reinforced with galvanized mesh and stitching wire, produced by spinning molten rock into fibers fused at 1520°C. Classified under EN 14303 as a mineral wool product, it is designed for thermal insulation in building equipment and industrial installations. It is treated with resins and hydrophobic agents to enhance cohesion, elasticity, and water resistance, and is formed into rolls and shrink-wrapped in PE film.

Key Advantages

  • High thermal and acoustic insulation
  • Fire-resistant and non-combustible
  • Maintains performance at high temperatures
  • Water-repellent, non-hygroscopic, and breathable
  • Inorganic, chemically inert, and suitable over stainless steel
  • Lightweight, easy to handle and install
  • Pest- and vibration-resistant
  • Recyclable and eco-friendly

Technical Specifications

  • Thicknesses: 30–120 mm
  • Roll width: 1000 mm
  • Length varies by thickness (e.g., 6 m for 30 mm)
  • Custom dimensions available

Applications

Ideal for thermal and acoustic insulation in building equipment and industrial installations.
