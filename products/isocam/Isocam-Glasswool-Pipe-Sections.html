Isocam Glasswool Pipe Sections

Isocam Glasswool Pipe Sections faced with Aluminum Foil are pre-formed insulation solutions made from high-quality glass wool, factory-laminated with a reflective aluminum foil. They are designed to provide effective thermal and acoustic insulation for pipework in various systems. The aluminum foil facing acts as a vapor barrier, improving moisture resistance and enhancing long-term durability. Lightweight and easy to install, these pipe sections deliver reliable performance in both chilled and hot pipe applications.

Technical Characteristics

  • Length: 1.2m
  • Density: 60 to 95 Kg/m3
  • Thermal Conductivity: 0.035 W/(mK)
  • Facing: Aluminum Foil
  • Available thickness: 25 to 100 mm.

Applications

  • Thermal insulation of hot and cold-water pipes, chilled water lines, and refrigeration systems
  • Acoustic insulation for HVAC ducting and piping
  • Suitable for commercial, industrial, and residential buildings
  • Ideal for above-ground and suspended piping systems where condensation control is important

Key Benefits

  • High thermal efficiency: Minimizes heat loss and reduces energy consumption
  • Acoustic insulation: Helps control noise from fluid movement and mechanical systems
  • Pre-formed and easy to install: Reduces installation time and labor costs
  • Aluminum foil facing: Acts as an effective vapor barrier and enhances durability
  • Non-combustible: Provides fire safety with a Euroclass rating (typically A2-s1,d0 or better)
  • Dimensional stability: Maintains form and performance over time

Sustainability

  • Made with recycled glass, reducing the use of raw materials
  • Fully recyclable at the end of its service life
  • Supports energy-efficient systems, contributing to reduced operational carbon emissions
  • Helps meet green building standards such as LEED and BREEAM by improving insulation and indoor air quality
