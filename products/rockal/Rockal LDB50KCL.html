Rockal LDB50KCL

Rockal LDB50KCL Rock wool board insulation is a rigid insulation material made from spun mineral wool, derived from volcanic rock and slag. It is known for its excellent thermal resistance, soundproofing capabilities, and fire-resistant properties.

Applications

  • Walls, roofs, and floors: Provides continuous insulation, improving energy efficiency
  • HVAC systems: Used in heating and cooling ducts to prevent heat loss
  • Power generator Canopies: Sound Insulation
  • Vertical insulation: cavity walls, ventilated facades, drywalls
  • Horizontal insulation: attics, floors, roofs, suspended ceilings
  • Construction components: acoustic-thermal-fireproof panels and doors

Advantages

  • Fire resistance: Non-combustible and meets strict fire safety standards
  • Soundproofing: Ideal for noisy areas like theaters and recording studios
  • Durability: Long-lasting and resistant to deterioration
  • Moisture and mold resistance: Works well in humid or damp conditions
  • Eco-friendly: Made from sustainable materials and recyclable
  • Easy installation: Suitable for contractors and DIY use
